Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • Quarter teaspoon white sugar
  • 3 quarter cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • Half a teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 110 degrees F (44 degrees C) in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. Combine 1 3/4 cups flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour yeast mixture; mix well until dough comes together.
  4. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a greased pizza pan.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, and bake. Enjoy!

To Bake:

Top crust as desired and bake at 500 degrees F (260 degrees C) until golden, 8 to 10 minutes.
